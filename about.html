Meet Caitlin

Caitlin attended world-renowned university, The University of Melbourne, ranked alongside institutions such as Harvard and Stanford. A BSc graduate with a major in psychology lends itself to the people aspect of real estate. Australia is known for its friendly, often funny and charming community and Caitlin carries these Australian philosophies into her everyday life in real estate.

Caitlin is a fourth-generation entrepreneur and third generation real estate professional. She grew up in her family's luxury fashion business, Scanlan Theodore, known for importing Italian silks and yarns and the first to import Christian Louboutin to Australia. She worked in all areas of the business until she was 24 and has founded her own businesses across various industries in both London and the US. This level of expertise comes with a breadth of experience related to real estate; finance, marketing, sales, design, customer service, management, negotiation and working with vendors. Fun Fact: Caitlin's luxury shoe brand made it into the top 100 businesses for a show featured on a major UK television network.

Caitlin has been living in San Francisco for 14+ years but has also lived in London, Rome and Florence. Caitlin's diverse personal, geographic, and business background makes her the perfect middle person during a transaction. Caitlin thinks outside of the box, thrives on negotiation and zealously advocates for her clients to get them the best terms possible.

Caitlin is preferred by clients who value active listening, empathy and careful attention to their needs. Her approach and style caters to those who want someone intuitive, who is a bold advocate for their interests, who also understands the importance of sharp analysis and direct communication about strengths and weaknesses of each purchase or sale.

Italy is Caitlin's home away from home. During her time off, you can find her there. She first learned salsa dancing in Italy and has been doing it ever since. Caitlin dabbles in the performing arts where she shares funny stories and performs stand up comedy on stage. She loves to write, create art and can cook some mean Italian and Lebanese cuisine.

Both buyers and sellers are thrilled to work with Caitlin because no detail goes missed. Give Caitlin a call today, at a minimum, to hear her endearing Australian accent.

My Approach

How Caitlin works with clients

Every client and every property is different — but the process is built on the same foundation of communication, preparation, and care.

1. Discovery Conversation

We start with an honest conversation about your goals, timeline, and priorities — no pressure, just planning.

2. Strategy & Search (or Prep)
