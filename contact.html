How Caitlin works with clients

Caitlin believes that real estate should be a collaborative, pressure-free experience. Whether you are buying your first home in San Francisco, selling a property you have loved for years, relocating from another city or country, or building an investment portfolio, Caitlin takes the time to understand your goals before offering any guidance.

Every client conversation starts with listening. Caitlin wants to understand what matters to you — your budget, your timeline, your preferred neighborhoods, your lifestyle needs — before making any recommendations. She is direct, honest, and will always tell you what she genuinely thinks rather than what you want to hear.

San Francisco is one of the most complex and nuanced real estate markets in the world. Prices, inventory, and micro market conditions can vary dramatically from one block to the next. Caitlin's deep local knowledge, third generation real estate background, and experience across Pacific Heights, Marina, Cow Hollow, Presidio Heights, Noe Valley, and all San Francisco neighborhoods means you have an informed advocate on your side from the very first conversation.

Call, email, or fill out the form — Caitlin personally responds to every enquiry. There is no pressure and no obligation. Just an honest conversation about what you are looking for and how she can best help you.
